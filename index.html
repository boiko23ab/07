About Us

Trusted Electric & HVAC Professionals

At North Heat & Air, you’re not just paying for a service — you’re choosing a team that takes your comfort personally. Since 2018, we’ve been helping homeowners and businesses across Washington State stay cozy in winter and cool in summer.

With years of hands-on experience and hundreds of completed projects, we know how to solve problems — even when the solution isn’t obvious. From the simplest repair to complex custom-home systems, we bring creativity, care, and proven technical expertise to every job.

We’re flexible, experienced, and solution-driven. Working with top manufacturers, we always choose what fits your space, your budget, and your long-term comfort best. And if the right solution doesn’t exist yet — we’ll create one.

We stand behind our work. All our labor is fully guaranteed, and all equipment and parts are covered by the manufacturer’s warranty. That means you’re protected — now and in the future. If you ever need us again, whether it’s next month or next year, we’ll be there.

Stay comfortable and confident with North Heat & Air — your trusted HVAC team in Washington State. Call us today to discover reliable solutions you can count on!
